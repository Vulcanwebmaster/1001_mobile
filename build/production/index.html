STANDARD EYE TEST

Regular eye tests are important for clear, comfortable vision. It is essential for your eye health as well. For the comprehensive eye check, it usually takes about 30-45mins.


During the test, our qualified optometrists will ask you simple questions regarding to your visual needs and health history. You will be required to read and perform various visual tasks in far and close distances.


After the tests, recommendations for your clear, comfortable vision and eye health will be made. These can include glasses or contact lenses, prescription eye drops, further specialised testing or review appointment in the future.


Comprehensive eye check is covered by Medicare every 24 months, and short eye check-ups in between.

CONTACT LENS CONSULTATION

Contact lenses can make a big difference to your life. It gives you clear, comfortable and natural vision – without any vision restrictions from frames. It is important to know that well-fitted contact lenses give you maximum comfort and clarity with minimum problems.


After general eye test, we will gather additional information so you can be fitted with best contact lenses for you.


First time wearers? Don’t worry, we will teach you how to put/remove contact lenses as well as how to take care of lenses.


For contact lens wearers, it is strongly recommended to get your eyes tested every 12 months to help maintain healthy eyes with clear and comfortable vision.
